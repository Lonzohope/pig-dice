PIG DICE

Rules

  1. This game involves two players

  2. Each player gets a chance to play

  3. In each round the player can see their scores

  4. If the dice rolls on 1 the player loses

  5. The first to score 100 points is the winner

  6. The user can hold the dice and the points would be added to the other scores

  7. For the first player click on the first play button

  8. For the second player click on the second play button

  9. For the first player to hold click on the first hold button

  10. For the second player to hold click on the second hold button
